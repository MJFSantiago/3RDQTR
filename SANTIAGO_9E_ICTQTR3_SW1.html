EVA 01
PILOT:Shinji Ikari
TEST-TYPE
!!WARNING!! BERSERKER MODE
BERSERKER MODE of EVA-01 is a powerful and dangerous mode that can be activated when the pilot, Shinji Ikari, is under extreme emotional distress or trauma. When the berserker mode is activated, EVA-01 becomes feral and uncontrollable, attacking anything and everything in its path. The berserker mode grants the Eva tremendous physical strength and endurance, but at the cost of its sanity and humanity. The berserker mode is a dangerous and uncontrolled state that can have devastating consequences, not only for the target, but also for those nearby. It is only used as an absolute last resort when all other weapons have failed, and the pilot has run out of options.

EVA 02
PILOT: Asuka Langley Sohryu (Briefly piloted by:Kaworu Nagisa)
FIRST EVANGELION BUILT SPECIFICALLY TO FIGHT THE ANGELS
!WARNING!: BEAST MODE
BEAST MODE: The beast mode of EVA-02 is a mode that can be activated by the pilot, Asuka Langley Soryu, during moments of extreme emotional stress or trauma. When the beast mode is activated, EVA-02 becomes uncontrolled and violent, attacking anything and everything in its path. The beast mode grants the Eva tremendous physical strength and endurance, but at the cost of its sanity and humanity. It is a dangerous and uncontrollable state that can have devastating consequences, not only for the target, but also for those nearby. The beast mode is a last resort for the pilot, only to be used when all other weapons have failed, and the pilot has run out of options. The beast mode is similar to the berserker mode of EVA-01 but with its own unique features, such as a horn that can stab and impale its enemies. The beast mode is extremely dangerous and should only be used as a last resort.
